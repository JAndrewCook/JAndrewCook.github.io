Java Applications: Project 5

Project 5 in our Java Applications course was to make a music playlist. We had to create the frames, most of the classes, and make it work. The design consisted of the list of songs, ordered by default in the order they were inserted into the array, buttons just to the left of the list so one could reorder the songs however they wished. A grid of buttons to the right of the list that would play the song it was associated with, the grid would also refresh as you edited the list of songs. Below the list there is a play/stop button, a skip forward and skip backward, as well as text saying what song was playing and who the artist was.
