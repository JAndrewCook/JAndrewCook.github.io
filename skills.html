What do they mean?

  • The personal skills are the list of skills I have that deal with how I can personally interact with a person or people.
  • Ther workplace skills is the list of skills I have that show how I interact and conduct myself in a workplace environment.
  • Technical skills are exactly that, the skills I have in a technical field that deal with how I use my skills on a technical level.
  • My hard skills are the list of skills that I can bring into a workplace environment that I can physically show in a portfolio.
  • My soft skills are the list of skills that I use in a conversation or team-based project. Things like communication, leadership and being self-paced.
